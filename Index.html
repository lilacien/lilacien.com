Design

Art...a form of release. An escape from the mundane to a place where all is possible, all is probable.

I exist trying to find the balance between a linear life and a life of untold promise. Here I have chosen to practice, show and enjoy all that I can create. Creation give me a sense of connection to the world. a way to share a part of me, otherwise hidden in th facades of my cultivated personality. But...ah the sweet release of the true me on a canvas, in a picture, on a tapestry. to release the pent up feelings and emotions. That is life.

CEO on less than $1 a day

Perhaps no one on our list (or anywhere) has been better at creating shareholder value than Apple‘s Steve Jobs. Since he rejoined the iconic computer maker in 1997, when Apple bought his NeXT Computer Inc. for its Unix-based operating system (the basis of the Mac’s OS X), Jobs has taken only $1 annually (don’t worry, thanks to Apple and Pixar, which he sold to Disney in 2006, Jobs is worth $8.3 billion.) For that buck, Jobs has generated an 8,333% return for Apple shareholders.
